🧠 The lesson from today. Every tempting bet died for the same three reasons, and they're worth knowing: (1) heavy favorites on Polymarket trade rich, not cheap — a 95¢ 'sure thing' usually risks 95¢ to make 5¢, so one surprise wipes out 20 wins; (2) if a fact is in the news, it's already in the price — that's the crowd, not an edge; (3) thin markets eat small edges through spread and slippage.
